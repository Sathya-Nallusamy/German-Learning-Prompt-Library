## ğŸŒŸ Quality Guidelines

### Good Prompts:
- Are specific and focused on one topic/skill
- Include clear instructions for the AI
- Have placeholder variables for customization (e.g., `{YOUR_LEVEL}`)
- Provide examples of expected output
- Are tested and verified to work

### Avoid:
- Overly complex prompts that try to do too much
- Prompts without clear structure
- Untested prompts
- Content that requires paid resources
