## âœ… A1 Completion Checklist

Before taking the exam, make sure you can:

- [ ] Introduce yourself (name, age, country, job)
- [ ] Talk about your family
- [ ] Describe your daily routine
- [ ] Order food at a restaurant
- [ ] Ask for directions
- [ ] Make simple requests

### Listening
- [ ] Understand simple announcements
- [ ] Follow slow, clear conversations
- [ ] Understand numbers and times

### Reading
- [ ] Read simple signs and notices
- [ ] Understand short messages and emails
- [ ] Follow simple instructions

- [ ] Fill out registration forms
- [ ] Write a short email (invitation, thank you)
- [ ] Write about yourself (50-60 words)

### Grammar
- [ ] Use present tense correctly
- [ ] Know basic articles (der, die, das)
- [ ] Use Nominativ and Akkusativ cases
- [ ] Form simple questions
- [ ] Use negation (nicht, kein)

### Vocabulary
- [ ] ~800 words across common topics
